FAQ

1. Can a team be consisting of students from different colleges?



Ans:- One team can have participants from multiple colleges.

2. Are there any restrictions on technologies used for development throughout the event?



Ans:- Participants are free to use any technology

3. What kind of problem statement can be expected?



Ans:- Problem statement category will be provided by Mentoring team.



4. Is referring to any source code from external sources allowed?



Ans:- Plagiarism is allowed up to 30%-40%.



5. For how long will the hackathon run?



Ans:- Duration of the event will be between 8 - 9 hrs.



6. What will happen if any doubt arises during the event?



Ans:- Assistance will be provided during the event if any issues occur.



7. What will happen in case of any IT or internet issues?



Ans:- Any IT glitches or internet problem will not be entertained.

8. When will the amount of registration fees be refunded?



Ans:- Registration amount will be refunded after the completion of the event. There will be no refund provided in case the team does not participate in the event.
