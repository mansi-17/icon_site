CHESS

About The Event

Chess is a game played between two opponents on opposite sides of a board containing 64 squares of alternating colors. Each player has 16 pieces: 1 king, 1 queen, 2 rooks, 2 bishops, 2 knights, and 8 pawns. The goal of the game is to checkmate the other king.

Event format:

  • Participants will have to make a chess.com account

  • Participants will be asked to send a challenge to his opponent

  • In case of a draw there will be "Armageddon"



Event rules:

  • It will be a 1v1 competition
  • Member should be college student.
