Event format:







Event rules:

  • 8 teams will participate in a single slot
  • All the members should be college students.
  • For valid participation all the team members should upload their ID Card scanned copy during the registration.
  • If the participant doesn't have an ID card, then fee receipt of the current year can be uploaded instead.
  • Players in each round would be flashed at the beginning of the round
  • All the teams will be given an equal amount to spend. The remaining amount at the end of the auction will not benefit you in winning
    • The bidding will take place based on the 2 teams to start bidding.
  • Time out of single bid will be for 10 seconds only
  • only one player from each team can take part in bidding system
  • In the occasion that one among the two teams backs out, other teams can opt to bid
  • The team with the highest bid at the end will add the player to their team
  • Only when the auctioneer calls out “sold,” will the bidding conclude
  • The teams must have a strength of 13 – 15 players
  • Any team can have a maximum of 8 foreign players and a minimum of 1 uncapped Indian player.
  • After all the rounds, each team will submit their list of players with captain and vice captain
  • The playing 11 must have 1 uncapped Indian player (Teams can be in the composition of 7 Indian players and 4 foreign players)
