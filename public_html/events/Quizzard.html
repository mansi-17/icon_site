About The Event

Bring your grey cells back to the party as ICON is back with its iconic quiz! This year more twist and turns are waiting for you. So get on board and have a one hell of an experience with us as we enter into the world of QUIZZARD.

Register here



Event format:

  • Round 1: General Question


    General Questions will be asked from all different categories.

  • Round 2: Guessing Round


    The team has to guess the answer by seeing the images presented.

  • Round 3: Bidding Point Round


    All the teams will bid the points they have earned as they see fit in order to win


Event rules:

  • It will be a team based competition.
  • In a team two members are allowed.
  • Both the members should be a college student.
  • For valid participation both the team member should upload their ID Card scanned copy during the registration.
  • If the participant doesn't have an ID card, then fee receipt of the current year can be uploaded instead.
