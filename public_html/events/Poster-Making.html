Poster Making

About The Event

Oh, look! It's time to get your innovative spirits up! We are with an excitingly cool challenge to help you do that! We bring you a poster competition but that's not all. There's a twist. The poster you make should be made using simple powerpoint presentation. Creativity is all about observation, imagination and innovation and adding a little challenge in that only maximizes the fun. So, buckle up and get ready for this!

Register here



Event format:

  • Round 1: THEME Reveal


    Themes will for making the poster will be provided to participants

  • Round 2: Process


    • Possibility 1: Participants will share the screen during the event. Volunteers will monitor the participant screen, and see if they are not breaking the rules and the guidelines.

    • Possibility 2:- If the participant is unable to share the screen, then he/she can record their whole screen while creating the poster and submit it.

  • Round 3: Submission


    As the participant will complete, he/she has to submit the .pptx file in the drive link, which will shared to them through the mail. After submitting they have to leave.



Event rules:

  • The member should be college students.
  • For valid participation member should upload their ID Card scanned copy during the registration.
  • If the participant doesn't have an ID card, then fee receipt of the current year can be uploaded instead.
  • Participants has to join 10 mins prior before the event starts.
  • No Graphics or images allowed from the web./No watermark images should be used and only free/open source images used.
  • 1 hr 30 mins maximum time constraint.
  • Can only use tools built into PowerPoint.
  • File has to save as .ppt extension.
  • ppt file should be uploaded into the google drive by using the given naming convention format
