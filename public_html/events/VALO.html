Valorant

About The Event

A 5v5 competitive online game, character-based tactical shooter game. 13 rounds to attack and defend your side.

Register here



Event format:

  • Knockout rounds until finals

  • Toss for selection of map, looser of the toss gets to choose which side to play (Attacking or Defending)

  • Finals will be best of 3 matches



Event rules:

  • It will be a team based competition.
  • In a team 5 members are allowed.
  • All the members should be college students.
  • For valid participation all the team member should upload their ID Card scanned copy during the registration.
  • If the participant doesn't have an ID card, then fee receipt of the current year can be uploaded instead.
