Market Marauders

About The Event

Oh, well, it’s time to get your management bug activated and jump deep into creativity and team spirit. Market Marauders has been especially designed to give you a taste of raiding into your inner self which comes up with all the cool ideas. All you have to do is complete a few tasks in synergy with your team and escape from elimination. That’s it! And you might just be able to steal the title of Market Marauders.

Register here



Event format:

  • Round 1: Fun facts


    Every team will be provided with 5 facts about a real world company. From the given facts, the participating team will have to guess the name of the company.

  • Round 2: Rethink. Redefine. Redesign.


    The participating team will be given an already existing product which they will have to redesign with respect to the future with the help of their creative bugs.

  • Round 3: Start creating!


    This round will come with an opportunity to let your imagination run free. Teams will have to design their own product!

  • Round 4: Advertise away!


    Participants will have to create an advertisement for the product which they have designed in round 3.


Event rules:

  • It will be a team based competition.
  • In a team four members are allowed.
  • All the members should be college students.
  • For valid participation all the team members should upload their ID Card scanned copy during the registration.
  • If the participant doesn't have an ID card, then fee receipt of the current year can be uploaded instead.
